Private Market Intelligence

What it is

Converts fragmented private markets documents and operational data into curated, trusted, Alpha-ready intelligence.

Why it matters

Private markets data sits across GP reports, capital calls, distribution notices, NAV statements, K-1s, Excel files, private credit loan tapes, valuation reports, and operational systems.

Demo story

A private markets analyst uploads GP reports and capital call notices. AI extracts fund, GP, LP, NAV, unfunded commitment, capital call amount, distribution, IRR, TVPI, DPI, valuation date, and exposure, then flags exceptions and publishes approved data.

Business benefits

Faster onboarding, reduced manual work, better NAV and exposure visibility, improved reconciliation, faster reporting, and source traceability.

Governed AI & Observability

What it is

Governance and observability control plane for GenAI, agentic AI, and ML.

Why it matters

Financial services AI needs traceability, access control, source grounding, prompt logging, human approval, monitoring, and auditability.

Demo story

User asks for a client-ready explanation of private markets exposure change. The assistant uses approved data, shows sources, logs prompt and response, highlights confidence, routes for approval, and stores the audit trail.

Business benefits

Safer AI adoption, lower hallucination risk, audit-ready AI, prompt and model monitoring, user trust, cost transparency, and scalable governance.

MLOps & ML Assistant

What it is

Industrialized AI/ML lifecycle with assistant-driven model operations.

Why it matters

AI/ML models need repeatable development, validation, deployment, monitoring, retraining, and governance.

Demo story

A risk or investment operations model loses accuracy. ML Assistant detects feature drift, identifies impacted portfolios or processes, recommends retraining, and creates a model health summary.

Business benefits

Faster deployment, standardized governance, better reliability, early drift detection, faster root cause analysis, and reduced manual monitoring.
